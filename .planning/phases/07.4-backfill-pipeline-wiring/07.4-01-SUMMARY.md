# Phase 07.4 Plan 01: SetBackfillEngine + SRC-06 Re-Snapshot Dispatch Summary

**One-liner:** Post-construction `SetBackfillEngine` setter breaks circular dependency and SRC-06 goroutine block triggers re-snapshot after replication slot loss.

## What Was Built

### Task 1: SetBackfillEngine method on PostgresConnector

Added `SetBackfillEngine(eng backfill.BackfillEngine)` exported method to `PostgresConnector` in `internal/source/postgres/connector.go` (after `EventLog()` accessor at line 180).

This breaks the circular dependency where `BackfillEngineImpl` needs `connector.AppendAndQueue` as its `appendFn`, but the constructor needed the engine. Pattern mirrors `BackfillEngineImpl.SetWatermark`.

### Task 2: SRC-06 re-snapshot dispatch block (12b)

Added block 12b immediately after block 12a in `connectAndStream`. When `needsSnapshot=true` and `backfillEng != nil`, launches a goroutine calling `backfillEng.Run(ctx)`. The block is explicitly placed after `StartReplication` to ensure slot and publication are confirmed present before snapshot queries begin.

Unlike block 12a, block 12b has no `HasPendingBackfills()` guard — slot loss implies full re-snapshot regardless of stored state.
