# Phase 07.4 Plan 02: BackfillEngine Wiring in runPipeline Summary

**One-liner:** BackfillEngineImpl wired into runPipeline with SQLiteBackfillStore, WatermarkChecker, and connector.AppendAndQueue as appendFn, activating the full snapshot/backfill flow.

## What Was Built

### Task 1: numEventLogPartitions constant and buildBackfillConfigs helper

Added `numEventLogPartitions = 64` constant to `internal/cmd/root.go` (after imports). This replaces two hardcoded `64` literals in `runPipeline` (at `eventlog.Open` and `router.NewRouter`) and is also used for `NewWatermarkChecker` — enforcing that all three always agree on partition count (BKF-02).

Added `buildBackfillConfigs(tables map[string]config.TableConfig, sourceID string) []backfill.BackfillConfig` package-level function. For each `tableKey` in the map, splits on `.` to derive `schema`/`table`, and produces a `BackfillConfig` with defaults: `Strategy="snapshot_and_stream"`, `PKCols=["id"]`, `NumPartitions=numEventLogPartitions`. Returns empty (not nil) slice for nil input.

### Task 2: BackfillEngineImpl construction and injection in runPipeline

Replaced the `// 10. Build Postgres connector (backfill engine nil for Phase 7.2).` block with a full 10a-10f construction sequence:

- **10a**: `PostgresConnector` created with nil engine placeholder (unchanged from before)
- **10b**: `SQLiteBackfillStore` opened at `cfg.DataDir/backfill.db` with `defer bkStore.Close()`
- **10c**: `buildBackfillConfigs` called to produce one `BackfillConfig` per configured table
- **10d**: `BackfillEngineImpl` created with `connector.AppendAndQueue` as `appendFn` and `pgx.Connect` closure as `openConnFn`
- **10e**: `WatermarkChecker` constructed with `el` and `numEventLogPartitions`; injected via `bkEng.SetWatermark(wc)`
- **10f**: `connector.SetBackfillEngine(bkEng)` breaks the circular dependency

Added `pgx/v5` and `backfill` package imports.
