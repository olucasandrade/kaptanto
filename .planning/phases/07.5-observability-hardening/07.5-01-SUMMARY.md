## Decisions Made

- `SetMetrics` uses post-construction injection matching the `SetBackfillEngine` pattern — avoids circular dependencies at construction time; all callers in `runPipeline` satisfy ordering before `Run`
- `checkWALLag` signature extended rather than using a closure — keeps the function testable as a pure function; `m` is nil-safe
- `ConsumerLag` semantics: Add(1) per skipped event on blocked path, Set(0) on first successful delivery — gives operators both a growing backlog signal and a "caught up" reset
